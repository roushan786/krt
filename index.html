About Us

Kettly is not like it’s other competitors but it presents the concept of tea tapri hygienically. Our tea centres won’t be styled unorthodoxly we will be styling them with Greek aesthetics for every location and centre. The tea we sell will be made by our own specially blended tea powder which comes from our own tea garden and tea powder trading company. We wouldn’t want to restrict your taste buds to just the traditional flavours but would offer other blends and flavours for you to savour. We intent to bring small business owners and big businessmen together and reach the international level. Kettly’s concept is that we want to convey the spirit of tea to the world. People think chai tapri’s are unhygienic, big stores are expensive and their tea is mixed with preservatives and additives, seeing this we thought of introducing kettly which is clean, hygienic and pocket-friendly. Our main objective is to introduce the world to flavourful tea.
